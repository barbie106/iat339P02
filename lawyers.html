DR. WILLIAM H. LIM

BACKGROUND

Dr. Lim is the founder and principal of Lim & Company and has over 30 years of experience in the practice of law in British Columbia, Canada. After receiving his Bachelor of Laws (LLB) in 1976 from the University of British Columbia, Dr. Lim was called to the British Columbia bar in July 1977 and has been in private practice in the Greater Vancouver area ever since. In 2004 Dr. Lim obtained his doctorate in business administration (DBA) from California Pacific University. Dr. Lim also has a Bachelor of Arts degree in Natural Science from Ateneo University (1972) in the Philippines.

AREAS OF PRACTICE

Dr. Lim's has extensive experience in a wide range of practice areas, which include real estate transactions, company law, commercial law, business law, immigration law, family law, wills and estate planning, trademark registration and criminal defence.

PROFESSIONAL AND COMMUNITY ACTIVITIES

Appointed by the Governor General of Canada and the Privy Council for Canada to serve as a Commissioner of the Fraser River Harbour Commission from 1995 to 2002.

GERALD K. MARTIN

BACKGROUND

Gerald was born and raised in Saskatchewan, he moved to B.C. in 1977 to pursue his chosen career in law. He had already received his Bachelor of Laws degree from the University of Saskatchewan in 1975, and that was in addition to a Bachelor of Science degree in Mathematics from the same university in 1970. After being called to the British Columbia bar in 1977, Gerald then entered the private practice of law in the Okanagan and subsequently in Vancouver.

AREAS OF PRACTICE

Gerald’s current practice is focused on corporate law, commercial law, real estate law and family law, with an emphasis on litigation. As a result, Gerald has had extensive courtroom experience at the trial and appellate levels, including Federal Court. He has also been actively involved in various arbitration and administrative tribunals.

PROFESSIONAL AND COMMUNITY ACTIVITIES

In his free time, Gerald enjoys recreational hockey, skiing and cycling. He continues to be in training for his first Tour de France (or so he says).

Languages Understood: English

JUSTIN B. LIM

BACKGROUND

Mr. Lim received his Bachelor of Laws (LLB) in 2001 from Osgoode Hall Law School of York University in Toronto, Ontario and was admitted to the British Columbia Bar in 2002. Prior to his law school studies, Mr. Lim studied at the University of British Columbia for two years.

AREAS OF PRACTICE

Mr. Lim’s areas of practice include company law, commercial law, business law, real estate law, trademark registration, wills and estate planning, and employment law. Mr. Lim manages the corporate law department of Lim & Company.
